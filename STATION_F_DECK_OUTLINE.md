# TodoMessenger STATION F Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1: Title

**TodoMessenger**

AI-powered workplace messaging that turns conversations into tasks.

Founder: Aniket Srivastava  
Location: Paris, France

## Slide 2: The Problem

Work tasks are hidden inside conversations.

Employees discuss work across:

- WhatsApp
- Teams
- Gmail
- Slack
- Zoom
- Google Meet
- informal chat

But action items are often never captured, assigned, or followed up.

Key message:

> Teams do not lose work because they lack tools. They lose work because tasks start in conversations and die there.

## Slide 3: Who Feels The Pain

Primary users:

- Employees who forget follow-ups.
- Team leads who manually chase people.
- Operations managers who lack visibility.
- Startups and SMEs where work is fast but process is informal.

Initial target:

Small and mid-sized teams with heavy communication volume and light operational structure.

## Slide 4: The Solution

TodoMessenger is a company messenger with tasks built directly into the conversation flow.

Core workflow:

1. Team chats naturally.
2. A message becomes a task.
3. Task gets assigned.
4. Due date/reminder is added.
5. Blu suggests missing follow-ups.
6. Everyone tracks work without leaving the conversation.

## Backup Slide: Risks And Mitigation

Risk: too close to existing messaging tools.  
Mitigation: focus on chat-to-task execution, not generic messaging.

Risk: privacy concerns around AI reading chats.  
Mitigation: explicit user controls, workspace admin policies, and transparent AI context windows.

Risk: too many integrations.  
Mitigation: start with one or two high-value channels: Gmail and Microsoft Teams or Google Meet.

Risk: enterprise security expectations.  
Mitigation: phased roadmap for SSO, audit logs, encryption, access controls, and data retention.
