# TodoMessenger Onboarding Video Script

## Video Goal

Create a 75 to 90 second onboarding video for first-time company users.

Audience:

- Company admin
- Team lead
- Employee

Main promise:

> Set up your workspace, invite your team, and turn chat into trackable work.

## Scene 1: Welcome

Duration: 0:00-0:07

On-screen text:

```text
Welcome to TodoMessenger
Work conversations. Real tasks.
```

Voiceover:

Welcome to TodoMessenger, the workplace messenger that helps teams turn conversations into assigned, trackable tasks.

## Scene 2: Sign In

Duration: 0:07-0:17

On-screen text:

```text
Step 1
Sign in with your work email
```

Voiceover:

Start with your work email. TodoMessenger checks your workspace and sends a verification code so the right people join the right company.

## Scene 3: Complete Profile

Duration: 0:17-0:26

On-screen text:

```text
Step 2
Add your name and status
```

Voiceover:

Complete your profile so teammates know who you are, what role you have, and whether you are available.

## Scene 4: Admin Workspace

Duration: 0:26-0:38

On-screen text:

```text
For admins
Create the workspace and invite employees
```

Voiceover:

Admins can open the Workspace tab, confirm the company domain, invite employees, and choose whether someone joins as an admin, team lead, or employee.

## Scene 5: Employee Home

Duration: 0:38-0:48

On-screen text:

```text
For employees
Start from recent chats
```

Voiceover:

Employees land directly in a clean chat interface, with recent conversations, tasks, and workspace access based on their role.

## Scene 6: Chat To Task

Duration: 0:48-1:01

On-screen text:

```text
Turn any message into a task
Assign it. Add a due date. Set a reminder.
```

Voiceover:

When a message becomes an action item, create a task directly from the chat. Assign it, add a due date, and set a reminder.

## Scene 7: Blu

Duration: 1:01-1:13

On-screen text:

```text
Ask Blu
@blu suggest tasks from this chat
```

Voiceover:

Blu can review approved chat context, suggest tasks, summarize decisions, and help teams catch follow-ups before they disappear.

## Scene 8: Integrations

Duration: 1:13-1:23

On-screen text:

```text
Connect the tools your company already uses
```

Voiceover:

TodoMessenger is designed to connect with the tools your company already uses, so work from chats, emails, and meetings can become one action layer.

## Scene 9: Closing

Duration: 1:23-1:30

On-screen text:

```text
You are ready
Chat naturally. Capture work automatically.
```

Voiceover:

You are ready. Chat naturally, capture work automatically, and keep every follow-up moving.
