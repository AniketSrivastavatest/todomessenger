FAQ

Is TodoMessenger a messaging app or a task management app?

It is both: a secure team messaging app where work can become assigned tasks, reminders, approvals, and tracked follow-ups.

Can TodoMessenger replace casual workplace chat?

TodoMessenger is built for teams that want familiar chat with stronger business workflow structure and workspace controls.

What makes Blu different from a normal chatbot?

Blu is designed around work context, action status, approval, reminders, and task outcomes instead of one-off answers.

Is TodoMessenger ready for enterprise buyers?

The product is moving toward enterprise readiness with authenticated workspaces, permission boundaries, audit records, and production persistence.
