# TodoMessenger Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: April 19, 2026

TodoMessenger is a messaging and task management app that helps users chat, create tasks, assign tasks, and use AI assistance for productivity.

## Information We Process

TodoMessenger may process account details, chat messages, tasks, assignees, invite links, and app usage information needed to provide the service.

## AI Features

When a user asks for AI help, such as using `@chatgpt` or asking the app to suggest tasks, selected chat or task context may be sent to the TodoMessenger backend and then to an AI provider to generate a response.

## Contact And Invite Features

TodoMessenger includes contact sync and invite prompts. Contact data should only be accessed after the user grants permission and should only be used to help users find or invite contacts.

## Storage

The Android app loads the TodoMessenger web app in a WebView. Some app data may be stored locally on the device using WebView storage so the app can remember chats, tasks, and settings.

## Third-Party Services

TodoMessenger may connect to services such as OpenAI, Asana, Jira, Netlify, and Render to provide AI, integration, hosting, and backend features.

## User Choices

Users can choose whether to use AI features, invite flows, and future app integrations. Users may clear local app data from Android system settings.
