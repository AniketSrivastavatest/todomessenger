Work does not always start in a project management tool. It starts in chat, email, and meetings.

Employees discuss what needs to be done, but action items are often never captured, assigned, or followed up.

TodoMessenger is an AI-powered workplace messenger that turns everyday conversations into trackable work.

Companies can create a workspace, invite employees, and give admins, team leads, and employees the right view.

When a message becomes an action item, users can create a task directly inside the chat.

Tasks can be assigned, prioritized, given due dates, and followed up with reminders.

Blu, the built-in AI assistant, reads recent chat context and suggests tasks, summaries, and follow ups.

The vision is to connect the tools employees already use into one action layer.

TodoMessenger makes workplace conversations actionable by default.
