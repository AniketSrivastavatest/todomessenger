Welcome to TodoMessenger, the workplace messenger that helps teams turn conversations into assigned, trackable tasks.

Start with your work email. TodoMessenger checks your workspace and sends a verification code so the right people join the right company.

Complete your profile so teammates know who you are, what role you have, and whether you are available.

Admins can open the Workspace tab, confirm the company domain, invite employees, and choose whether someone joins as an admin, team lead, or employee.

Employees land directly in a clean chat interface, with recent conversations, tasks, and workspace access based on their role.

When a message becomes an action item, create a task directly from the chat. Assign it, add a due date, and set a reminder.

Blu can review approved chat context, suggest tasks, summarize decisions, and help teams catch follow-ups before they disappear.

TodoMessenger is designed to connect with the tools your company already uses, so work from chats, emails, and meetings can become one action layer.

You are ready. Chat naturally, capture work automatically, and keep every follow-up moving.
